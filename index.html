Who We Are

Trusted guides for Thar, Karoonjhar, Northern Pakistan, Kashmir & All-Pakistan Tours

Thar Karoonjhar Travel & Tour is a trusted local travel agency based in Mithi, specializing in tours across Sindh, Kashmir, Gilgit, Hunza and Skardu. Our mission is to provide immersive, sustainable and unforgettable experiences that celebrate Pakistan’s natural beauty and cultural heritage.

Whether you want a serene desert safari, an alpine lake trek, or a cultural immersion, our team will craft the perfect itinerary for you.
