Who We Are

Your Gateway to Thar, Northern Pakistan, Kashmir & Beyond

Thar Karoonjhar Travel & Tour is a trusted local travel agency based in Mithi, specializing in tours across the Thar Desert and the picturesque Karoonjhar Mountains. Our mission is to provide immersive, sustainable and unforgettable experiences that celebrate the region’s natural beauty and rich cultural heritage. With local expertise and a passion for adventure, we curate journeys that connect you with desert landscapes, vibrant traditions and friendly communities.

Whether you’re looking for a serene desert safari, a challenging trek through granite hills or a cultural immersion into Sindhi life, our team will craft the perfect itinerary for you. Join us to explore hidden gems, witness stunning sunsets over shifting dunes and share stories under starry skies.
